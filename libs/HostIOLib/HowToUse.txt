Until this becomes a formal library, you can pull out individual .h/.c combinations
and directly link them into your application. 

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To use xboxHostIO.h/c:

Note: This will introduce a dependency on xbdm.dll and msvcp71.dll to your executable.

Add to "Configuration Properties / General / Additional Include Directories":

    ../../3rdparty/xdk/win32;../../libs/HostIOLib;

Add to "Configuration Properties / Linker / General / Additional Library Directories":

    ../../3rdparty/xdk/win32lib;
